Biography

Linux Torvalds is a Finnish-American software engineer who is the creator and principal developer of the Linux krenel,which is the kernel for a number of operating systems.He also created the version control system Git.Torvalds was born on December 28,1969 in Helsinki,Finland.He studied computer science at the University of Helsinki,and he developed the Linux kernel at the study project in 1991.Since then,the Linux kernel has become one of the most widely used kernels in the world, powering everything from smartphones to smartcomputers.
